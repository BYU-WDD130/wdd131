Red Rose Dessert

Site Name

Red Rose Dessert – This name symbolizes elegance, beauty, and indulgence. The “Red Rose” suggests passion and delicacy, while “Dessert” clearly communicates the focus of the bakery: handcrafted sweets and pastries. It’s unique, memorable, and visually appealing.

Suggested domain: redrosedessert.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Red Rose Dessert website is to showcase its wide range of artisanal desserts, display seasonal offerings, allow customers to place special custom cake orders, and provide important business information such as hours, location, and contact forms. The site will also feature a blog with baking tips and event announcements.

Scenarios

  • How do I place a custom cake order and check pricing?
  • What seasonal treats are available this month at Red Rose Dessert?

Color Scheme

  • #c2185b – Rose Pink: Used for headings, logo elements, and buttons.
  • #fff8f5 – Soft Blush: Used as the background color for a warm and inviting look.
  • #5d4037 – Rich Cocoa: Used for text and accent details.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for headings to give a classy and elegant impression.
  • Open Sans – Used for body text to ensure readability and simplicity.
